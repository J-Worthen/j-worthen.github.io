Bioinformatics & Genomics

Transcript-level gene regulation | Circadian biology | Alternative splicing

I am a bioinformatician and molecular biologist studying transcript-level gene regulation. My work focuses on circadian biology, alternative splicing, and isoform dynamics, using RNA-seq and computational approaches to uncover regulatory mechanisms not visible at the gene level.
